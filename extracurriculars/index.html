Extracurriculars and Interests

Some academia-related activities that I have been involved in (outside of my field of study) took place at my community college. This included participation in the Honors Program, where I met with professors to discuss topics outside the scope of the class and wrote research papers on issues such as human trafficking and mythological female heroes. I also spent months preparing a presentation for a panel that explored intersectional feminism and female criticisms.

A few of my interests involve reading murder mysteries, watching psychological thrillers, and traveling.

Volunteerism

Volunteering is one of many activities that I have always found very uplifting. One of my favorite volunteer activities has been working at a gift shop in a hospital, where I maintained the store and delivered gifts to the sick. Another volunteer experience that I loved partaking in was at my local mosque, where I helped prepare meals over the weekends for local homeless shelters in Chicago.
